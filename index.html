About Cellfix

At Cellfix, we pride ourselves on being Mumbai's leading destination for mobile and watch repairs. Located conveniently near Vakola, Santacruz East, we've been serving the community with dedication and expertise for years.

Our mission is simple: to provide unparalleled repair services that bring your devices back to life quickly and efficiently. We understand the importance of your mobile phones and watches in your daily routine.

From cracked screens and battery issues to complex internal diagnostics, our team is equipped to handle a wide array of problems with precision and care.

Beyond Repairs: Our Premium Accessories

Enhance your mobile and watch experience with our curated selection of high-quality accessories. We believe that proper accessories are crucial for protecting your devices and maximizing their functionality.

Our inventory includes everything from robust phone cases and crystal-clear screen protectors to fast-charging cables, power banks, and a wide array of headphones and earphones for superior audio.

For watches, we offer stylish straps in various materials, protective cases, and tools for minor adjustments, ensuring your timepiece is always ready for your lifestyle. All our accessories are sourced for their durability and performance.
